About Riyadh

Riyadh Riyadh is the capital of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, its largest city and the third most populous Arab capital. Riyadh is located in the center of the Arabian Peninsula in the plateau of Najd, at an altitude of 600 meters above sea level. It is the headquarters of the Emirate of Riyadh Region, according to the administrative division of the Saudi regions. About 10.5 million people live in the city of Riyadh.
